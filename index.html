BIO

Helen of Joy is fronted by guitarist Michal Michalik and singer Jessica Royea. In 2014, Jess and Mike met to form an acoustic duo and played covers for a year before deciding to write their own music. In Fall 2015, they added a drummer and keyboardist to help build the sound they were now creating.
